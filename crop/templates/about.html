🌱 Crop Prediction

In the Crop Prediction tool, we help you find the perfect crop for your agricultural land by analyzing critical soil and weather parameters. Simply input your land’s Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), pH, Humidity, and local weather data like Temperature 🌡️ and Rainfall 🌧️. Once you submit these details, our model will predict the most suitable crop that matches the given conditions—ensuring you can maximize your land’s potential.

🌾 Crop Recommendation

Our Crop Recommendation feature goes a step further by using real-time weather data 🌦️. All you need to provide are your soil details like Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), soil pH, and Humidity, along with your District and State. With this information, we use the OpenWeather API to fetch live weather conditions and recommend several crops best suited to grow in your area’s current climate and soil.
